You will be assigned to a different group of participants each round, chosen randomly. There will always be a total of {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants in your group, including yourself.

In each round, you will be given a certain starting amount . This amount may change between rounds. You will have the option to give some or all of your starting amount to your group. You will choose how much to give by moving a slider. The initial position of the slider will be set randomly each round.

You will have 1 minute, 15 seconds to make a decision in each round. If time runs out, whatever the slider currently shows will be submitted as your choice.

Any points you choose to give will be multiplied by {{ donation_multiplier }}, and then shared equally with everyone in the group, including yourself. This means that for each {{ 1 | c }} you choose to give, you will get back {{ Constants.MPCR_nonprofit | c}}.
