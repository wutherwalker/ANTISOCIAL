Doctors Without Borders/Medecins Sans Frontieres(MSF) is an organization that helps people worldwide where the need is greatest, delivering emergency medical aid to people affects by conflict, epidemics, disasters, or exclusion from health care. In emergencies and their aftermath, Medecins Sans Frontieres provides basic health care, rehabilitates and runs hospitals and clinics, performs surgery, battles epidemics, carries out vaccination campaigns, provides water, sanitation, and shelter support, runs nutritional programs, and offers mental health care. Through longer-term programs, Medecins Sans Frontieres also treats chronic diseases such as tuberculosis, malaria, sleeping sickness, and AIDS.
