You will be assigned randomly to a sender or to a receiver and your role will remain fixed for all rounds.

In this part of the experiment, both a sender and a receiver can see each other’s group affiliation and what product, if any, the other player bought.

Role of Sender: The Sender will be given {{ endowment }} points and may choose to send any amount of this money to the Receiver. The amount chosen by the Sender will be multipled by {{ multiplier }} and given to the Receiver.

Role of Receiver: The Receiver, upon learning how much money is available to him or her, will be able to send any amount of that money back to the Sender.

Earnings in each round:

The Sender earns {{ endowment }} - (points sent to Receiver) + (points sent back by Receiver).

The Receiver earns {{ multiplier }} × (points sent by Sender) – (points sent back to Sender).

The earnings in each round will not be revealed until the experiment ends.
