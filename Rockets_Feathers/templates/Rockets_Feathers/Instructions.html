Welcome to our experiment. In this experiment you will be in a group of {{ market_size }} participants; each of you will be acting in the role of a firm setting prices.

Your firms sell similar, but not identical products. If your price is higher than the average price, you will lose some customers, but not all of them (unless the difference is very large). If your price is lower than the average price, you will gain some customers, but again not all of them (unless the difference is very large).

Market conditions will change over time. In each round, you will be given a payoff table indicating what your profits will be given the prices you and other participants choose. This payoff table may be the same or different between consecutive rounds, so read carefully.

One round will be randomly chosen to be the paying round. Your profits from that round will be converted into dollars at a rate of {{ point_value }} per point.
