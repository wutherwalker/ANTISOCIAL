  • The black items (i.e., your preferences) will be fixed and cannot be changed
  • The red items (i.e., your model of your partner's preferences) will be possible to adjust at any time
  • The red items do not move independently of each other. If you move one red item, the computer will move all other red items at the same time so that the relationship between them do not change
  • The computer has information about the relationship between the items in the model of your partner. You will provide the computer with information on how your model of your own preferences compares with your model of your partner's preferences

An example using sports is shown to the right. This example is only for demonstration purposes. You can change the red items by clicking on one of the labels and dragging it up (to increase liking) or down (to decrease liking).
